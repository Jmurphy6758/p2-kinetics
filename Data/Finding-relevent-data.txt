1. University of Michigan 
   * R0 value of COVID-19 is between 1.5 and 3.5 
   website: (https://labblog.uofmhealth.org/rounds/how-scientists-quantify-intensity-of-an-outbreak-like-covid-19)

2. John Hopkins University Whiting School of Engineering 
   * Infection data from Johns Hopkins University both world wide data and data by state (on their git hub)
   website: (https://github.com/CSSEGISandData/COVID-19/blob/master/csse_covid_19_data/csse_covid_19_time_series/time_series_covid19_confirmed_US.csv)

3. New York Times
   * Infection Data for Idaho that includes total death,total cases, per capita, and new cases
   website: (https://www.nytimes.com/interactive/2020/us/idaho-coronavirus-cases.html)

4. Idaho Statesman
   * Data on the total number of deaths and deaths that occured each day 
   website(https://www.idahostatesman.com/news/coronavirus/article242127516.html)

5. Idaho Division of Public Health
   * The collection of data of all of Idaho on the number of confirmed COVID-19 cases. Includes data on the number of confirmed cases that occur each day, by age group, by sex, etc. 
   website: (https://public.tableau.com/profile/idaho.division.of.public.health#!/vizhome/DPHIdahoCOVID-19Dashboard_V2/Story1)

6. John Hopkins University and Medicine 
   * COVID-19 data based on each countryin every state has reported 
   website: (https://coronavirus.jhu.edu/us-map)

   website: (https://towardsdatascience.com/infectious-disease-modelling-part-i-understanding-sir-28d60e29fdfc)
 	    (https://towardsdatascience.com/infectious-disease-modelling-beyond-the-basic-sir-model-216369c584c4)
	    (https://towardsdatascience.com/infectious-disease-modelling-fit-your-model-to-coronavirus-data-2568e672dbc7)
	    (https://github.com/hf2000510/infectious_disease_modelling/blob/master/part_two.ipynb0)

8. International Journal of Infections Diseases
   *  Monitoring Transmissibility and Mortality of COVID-19 in Europe: Table of R(t) values over time in different European countries, including Italy
   website: (https://www.ijidonline.com/article/S1201-9712(20)30182-X/fulltext)

9. KTVB 7

    * Up to date data visualitations which include data from the global map
